Plant Directory

Pothos

An easy to care for, but poisonous, this trailing plant called Devil's Ivy is beginner friendly.

Peperomia

A very large plant family comprised of over 1,000 species, beginner friendly and tropical.

Ficus

These plants are frequently used in offices with low light, and are also known to improve air quality.

Philodendron

Able to survive in all sorts of lighting and watering conditions, these plants adapt to indoor life well.

Syngonium

Flowering tropical plant family with many distinct and beautiful varities for indoor decoration.

Marantaceae

Also known as arrowroots, these plants are known for their movement throughout the day as they follow the light.

Monstera

These large tropical plants typically grow around large trees, and have become very popular in the plant trade.
